About This Website "Le Guide Ann Arbor"

Welcome to my website!! This is an Ann Arbor restaurant rating site purely based on the personal dinning experience on that specific visit. As a food lover, I will give objective critics and rating based on the environment, service, taste, and price. I will rate all 4 aspects in a total score of 5 (the higher the better). Finally, the average of all 4 scores will become the overall score. Also, I will list my recommended dishes based on personal preference.

About Myself
